# Healthcare Customer Success Playbook

A practical portfolio playbook for onboarding, adoption, risk management, executive business reviews, stakeholder alignment, and 30/60/90-day planning in healthcare technology and services.

## What this project demonstrates

- Structured implementation and onboarding
- Customer adoption measurement
- Risk identification and recovery planning
- Customer health scoring
- Executive QBR preparation
- Stakeholder mapping
- Outcome-based success planning
- Healthcare-aware communication and governance

## Guiding principle

Customer success is not “checking in.” It is a disciplined operating system that connects adoption, stakeholder confidence, measurable outcomes, risk reduction, and the customer’s business priorities.
