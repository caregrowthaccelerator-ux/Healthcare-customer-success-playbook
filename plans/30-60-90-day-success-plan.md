# 30/60/90-Day Customer Success Plan

## Days 1–30: Align and establish the baseline

- Confirm outcomes, scope, owners, and governance
- Complete stakeholder map
- Define success metrics and data sources
- Validate workflow and implementation dependencies
- Establish communication and escalation cadence
- Capture baseline adoption and operational performance

**Deliverables:** Success plan, stakeholder map, risk log, launch checklist

## Days 31–60: Drive adoption and remove friction

- Monitor priority-user activation
- Coach users on the highest-value workflow
- Identify recurring support and usability issues
- Expand adoption beyond the initial champion
- Review progress with operational leadership
- Create recovery plans for risk signals

**Deliverables:** Adoption report, enablement plan, issue themes, recovery plans

## Days 61–90: Prove value and plan expansion

- Measure progress against baseline
- Prepare executive business review
- Confirm value narrative with the sponsor
- Prioritize optimization opportunities
- Document expansion or additional-use-case readiness
- Establish the next-quarter roadmap

**Deliverables:** QBR, value summary, optimization backlog, next-quarter plan
