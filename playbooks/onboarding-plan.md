# Healthcare Customer Onboarding Plan

## Phase 1: Alignment

### Objectives

- Confirm business outcomes
- Define scope and responsibilities
- Identify executive sponsor and operational owner
- Confirm privacy, security, and compliance requirements
- Establish communication cadence

### Exit criteria

- Success outcomes documented
- Stakeholder map completed
- Implementation owner assigned
- Risks and dependencies logged
- Timeline approved

## Phase 2: Configuration and readiness

### Objectives

- Validate workflows
- Complete data and integration prerequisites
- Configure roles and permissions
- Prepare training environment
- Confirm support and escalation channels

### Exit criteria

- Critical configuration tested
- Required users identified
- Training schedule confirmed
- Go-live checklist approved

## Phase 3: Launch

### Objectives

- Train priority user groups
- Monitor first-use activity
- Resolve launch blockers
- Communicate progress to stakeholders

### Exit criteria

- Core workflow completed successfully
- Priority users activated
- Launch issues assigned and tracked
- Executive sponsor updated

## Phase 4: Adoption and optimization

### Objectives

- Measure behavior, not only attendance
- Identify low-adoption groups
- Reinforce use cases
- Connect usage to business outcomes
- Build an optimization backlog

### Exit criteria

- Baseline adoption established
- Success metrics reviewed
- Recovery plans created for at-risk areas
- QBR date scheduled
