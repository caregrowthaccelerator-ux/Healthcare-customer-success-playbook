# Stakeholder Map

| Stakeholder role | Influence | Interest | Primary goal | Concern | Engagement plan |
|---|---:|---:|---|---|---|
| Executive sponsor | High | Medium | Business outcome | Strategic value | Monthly executive update |
| Operational owner | High | High | Reliable workflow | Capacity and adoption | Weekly working session |
| Clinical leader | High | High | Quality and fit | Workflow burden | Clinical-use-case review |
| IT or integration lead | High | Medium | Security and reliability | Technical dependency | Technical checkpoint |
| Compliance or privacy | High | Medium | Appropriate controls | Data handling | Governance review |
| Frontline champion | Medium | High | Ease of use | Training and friction | Office hours and feedback |
| Finance or procurement | Medium | Low | Contract value | Budget | Milestone-based value summary |

## Stakeholder questions

- Who can approve a decision?
- Who can block implementation?
- Who experiences the workflow daily?
- Who owns the measurable outcome?
- Who needs evidence of value?
- Who has recently changed roles?
- Where is the relationship concentrated in only one person?
