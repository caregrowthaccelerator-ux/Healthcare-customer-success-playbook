# Synthetic Account Plan

All organizations, people, and metrics in this example are fictional.

## Account

**Customer:** Harbor Health Network  
**Product:** Care-coordination workflow platform  
**Stage:** Post-launch adoption  
**Health:** 74% — Watch

## Evidence

- Strong executive sponsor
- Four of six priority teams actively using the platform
- Core workflow completion improving
- One facility has low repeat usage
- Reporting integration is delayed
- Outcome measurement has started but is not yet validated

## Top risk

Value is concentrated in two highly engaged champions. The broader user base has uneven adoption.

## 30-day recovery actions

1. Conduct workflow observation at the low-adoption facility.
2. Identify a secondary champion.
3. Deliver role-based refresher training.
4. Resolve reporting ownership.
5. Present adoption trend and next actions to the sponsor.

## Success signal

Repeat weekly use increases across the low-adoption facility, and outcome reporting has a named owner and confirmed data source.
